ABOUT

Hey, what’s up? Listen here right quick for somethin sultry for your ear holes. We had an idea and thought you’d be into it. We wanted to sit down with like minded individuals for an open conversation on the reasoning we gravitate between two cultures - skateboarding and motorcycles. We can’t speak for everyone in these lifestyles but we can confidently tell you we wouldn’t be the same without them. The way we carry ourselves, the way we dress, music taste and even the friends we made along the way. As kids, we always wanted to be “cool” and motorcycles and skateboards are cool as fuck to us. We believe you might feel the same. If you do, check out our podcast @kickflipsandkickstands where we’ll talk about bikes and boards and anything in between. Coming soon to wherever you get your podcast fix.
